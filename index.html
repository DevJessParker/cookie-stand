It all started with a simple Chinookie. While walking down a busy coatal boardwalk, Pete Fellows took a shortcut between a odorous, foul-smelling fishmarket and an upscale French-inspired bakery. Inspiration struck.

The community loved seafood.

They also loved delicious baked goods.

Why not fill an underserved market by combining both Chinook salmon and French scone into one extraordinary morsel for the most discerning of palettes? What started as an ugly pastry became something more. Something legendary.

Thus, Pete's Salmon Cookies were born.
